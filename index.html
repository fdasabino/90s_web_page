About

Ayrton Senna da Silva was a Brazilian racing driver who won the Formula One World Drivers' Championship in 1988, 1990, and 1991. Senna is one of three Formula One drivers from Brazil to win the World Championship and won 41 Grands Prix and 65 pole positions, with the latter being the record until 2006.

The life of a legend

In 2009 Ayrton Senna has been voted the greatest Formula 1 driver of all time by poll of 217 of his fellow world championship drivers including sir Lewis Hamilton.

Standing records

Senna is the King of Monaco, with 6 wins in the Principality, a record that still stands today in F1. Making him only Brazilian driver who has ever won the race.

A racing master

As the King of Pole-Positions, Ayrton Senna tended to win every time he started in the first, second or third row. But starting from the back never stopped him from winning the race.
